Privacy Policy - Voice Viva

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Voice Viva, a digital viva and voice-based academic evaluation platform designed to help students practice and participate in viva examinations using voice interaction and AI assistance.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect.
  • How your information is used.
  • How we protect your data.
  • Your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use Voice Viva, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Student ID (if provided by institution)
  • Profile information
  • Institution / department details (if applicable)

1.2 Voice Data

Voice Viva allows users to answer viva questions using voice. We may temporarily process or store:

  • Voice recordings
  • Speech-to-text transcripts
  • Response timestamps

Voice data is used only for evaluation, analysis, and improving the learning experience.

1.3 Usage Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Session duration
  • Error logs and diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow students to participate in digital viva sessions
  • Convert voice answers into text using speech recognition
  • Analyze responses for academic evaluation
  • Improve AI models and app performance
  • Provide updates and important notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement security measures to protect your information including:

  • Secure server communication (HTTPS)
  • Authentication systems for secure login
  • Restricted database access
  • Regular monitoring and security updates

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services. Voice recordings and transcripts may be stored temporarily for evaluation purposes and may be deleted after assessment completion.

5. Third-Party Services

Voice Viva may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (authentication and database services)
  • Cloud hosting services
  • Speech recognition services

These providers follow strict security standards to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Voice Viva is intended for educational use by students and institutions. Users under 13 should only use the app under supervision of an educational institution or guardian.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with the revised date.
